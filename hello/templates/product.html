What Is P2P Financing

Peer-to-Peer financing, also known as crowd-funding, is a revolutionary financial model that facilitates direct borrowing and lending between individuals or businesses through an online platform without the involvement of intermediaries and incumbents.

Who Are The Investors?

In peer-to-peer financing, any institution or individual who wants to maximize their gains can be an investor.

Who Are The Borrowers?

Generally, any individual or business looking for financing can be a potential borrower in peer-to-peer financing.
