Who are We?

Finja Invest is at the forefront of transforming businesses through purpose-driven financing solutions. Our investment portfolio extends across diverse industries, including the essential supply chains of Fast-Moving Consumer Goods (FMCG), Agriculture, and Logistics. Additionally, for institutional or individual borrowers outside of the supply chain ecosystem, Finja also provides finances against Gold. With a closed-loop and purpose-built financing model, Finja Invest empowers businesses to attain unparalleled financial stability. Our commitment lies in strategically supporting these vital sectors, enabling them to thrive and contribute to the nation's economic growth. Through tailored investment strategies, we facilitate sustainable growth and foster a brighter future for businesses within these dynamic industries.

Agriculture Supply Chain

Finja demonstrates its unwavering commitment to enhancing the Agriculture supply chain by extending invaluable support to distributors and manufacturers. Our innovative approach involves providing comprehensive invoice discounting solutions specifically designed for Distributors and Wholesalers. This strategic financial support ensures these vital players in the agricultural ecosystem have convenient and swift access to funds. These funds are earmarked for securing essential agricultural supplies, thereby facilitating the uninterrupted flow of agricultural activities.

FMCG Supply Chain

Finja Invest maintains a core focus on the Fast Moving Consumer Goods (FMCG) supply chain industry, where our closed-loop financing solutions play a pivotal role. Our digital credit offerings empower retailers with the means to purchase stock, fueling their growth and operational agility efficiently. In addition, we offer invoice discounting solutions tailored for distributors and wholesalers, enabling them to optimize cash flow by leveraging their stock purchases.

Gold-Backed Financing

Beyond the supply chain ecosystems, Finja, in partnership with Goldfin, extends its financial support to institutional or individual borrowers through a unique avenue: financing against gold. This innovative offering provides borrowers with an opportunity to leverage their gold assets to secure the funds they need to drive business growth. Additionally, investors have the option to participate in this portfolio, allowing them to diversify their investment strategy. Borrowers benefit by utilizing the acquired funds to expand their ventures while simultaneously honoring their financial commitments by returning the capital along with the accrued returns to the investors. Once the obligation is met, borrowers regain ownership of their gold, completing a seamless and mutually beneficial transaction.

Logistics Supply Chain

Finja showcases its unwavering dedication to fortifying the logistics supply chain by offering invaluable assistance to logistics service providers and transportation companies. Our progressive strategy entails delivering comprehensive invoice financing solutions tailored explicitly for the logistics sector. This strategic financial backing guarantees that these essential participants in the logistics network enjoy convenient and prompt access to capital.

Telecommunications Franchises & Retail Financing

Finja in partnership with some major players in the telecommunications industry plans on expanding it’s investment portfolio in the telco industry through tailored financial solutions for the businesses involved in the segment.

Industrial Engineering Supply Chain

Finja exemplifies its steadfast dedication to strengthening the industrial engineering supply chain by offering invaluable support to manufacturers and suppliers. Our innovative approach encompasses providing comprehensive invoice discounting solutions tailored specifically for industrial engineering distributors and producers.
