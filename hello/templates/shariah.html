Who are We?

Finja Invest is at the forefront of transforming businesses through purpose-driven financing solutions. Our investment portfolio extends across diverse industries, including the essential supply chains of Fast-Moving Consumer Goods (FMCG), Agriculture, and Logistics. Additionally, for institutional or individual borrowers outside of the supply chain ecosystem, Finja also provides finances against Gold. With a closed-loop and purpose-built financing model, Finja Invest empowers businesses to attain unparalleled financial stability. Our commitment lies in strategically supporting these vital sectors, enabling them to thrive and contribute to the nation's economic growth. Through tailored investment strategies, we facilitate sustainable growth and foster a brighter future for businesses within these dynamic industries.

How is our Financing based on Islamic Principles?

We operate as a Shariah-compliant investment platform, making us a trusted financial partner for those seeking ethical investment opportunities. Instead of disbursing cash, we provide financing against the stock the borrower purchases or for the invoice the borrower seeks to discount. This rigorous oversight not only guarantees the ethical purity of our financing but also provides peace of mind to our valued investors, assuring them that their investments are conducted within the bounds of Islamic ethics. We consider Shariah compliance as not just a requirement but a fundamental element that underpins our entire approach to responsible and ethical financing.
